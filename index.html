How it works
Three Steps. Zero Effort.
STEP 01
🗺️
Auto-Detect Route
The extension reads the origin and destination directly from your TMS form — no copy-paste, no re-entry. Works on Rose Rocket, Samsara, Motive, Geotab and more.
STEP 02
📡
Map the Real Highway
Geocodes both endpoints, then calls the OSRM routing engine to trace the actual highway. Samples 6 GPS waypoints along the route so Claude knows exactly which towns you'll pass through.
STEP 03
⚡
AI Ranks Your Fuel Stops
Claude analyzes prices at stations near each waypoint, calculates net savings after detour cost, and ranks by value — not just lowest price. A $1.75/L station 60km off-route ranks below a $1.81/L station on the highway. Reefer savings calculated separately.
STEP 04
💰
See Fleet-Wide Savings
Flip to the Savings tab for a live rollup — per truck, per fill, weekly, monthly, and annual projections for your entire fleet. Updates instantly when you change fleet size or fill frequency.
Built for Canadian Fleet Operations
Every Feature Dispatcher-First
🔌
Works Inside Your TMS
A slim sidebar slides into Rose Rocket, Samsara, Motive, Geotab, KeepTruckin, Omnitracs, FleetComplete, Trimble, and ReeférTek — no tab switching, no separate app.
🛣️
Real Highway Routing
Uses free OSRM routing (same engine as OSRM.org) to trace the actual Trans-Canada or US Interstate path. Stations are on your route, not in the wrong direction.
🤖
Claude AI Analysis
Powered by Anthropic's Claude. Understands detour cost vs. price savings — won't recommend a cheap station that wastes more in fuel getting there.
⛽
Diesel + Gasoline
Handles long-haul diesel rigs and lighter gasoline fleets. Tracks DEF availability, truck stop amenities, cardlock stations, and commercial discount lanes.
🧊
Reefer Unit Optimization
Enable the reefer toggle to track your temperature unit's red dyed diesel separately. Tax-exempt savings calculated independently — the avg $0.15/L tax advantage is factored into every stop recommendation.
🔑
Your Key, Your Data
Connects directly to Anthropic using your own API key. Zero routing through third-party servers — route data and fuel analysis stay between you and Claude.
📋
Dispatch Log & Annualized ROI
Every confirmed fuel stop is logged. The Log tab builds a running record of actual dispatches and projects annualized savings from real data — shareable with management at any time.
📊
Fleet Savings Dashboard
Enter your fleet size and fill frequency once. Get live projections: weekly, monthly, and annual savings across your entire fleet — ready for management reporting.
